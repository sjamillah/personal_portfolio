WHO AM I?

I'm SSOZI Jamillah, and my love for technology began when I was just a curious kid, fascinated by the glow of computer screens and the thrill of coding. I spent hours tinkering with computers, amazed by the endless things they could do. Each time I pressed a key, it felt like I was opening a door to a world where anything was possible.

Now, I'm a student at African Leadership University (ALU) in Kigali, Rwanda, studying Software Engineering. Here, surrounded by the bustling energy of campus life, I'm on a mission to learn all about data science and full-stack web development. From the moment I arrived at ALU, I knew I was in for an incredible adventure. With determination in my heart and a passion for technology driving me forward, I threw myself into my studies. I've learned so much—from mastering Python and Java to exploring HTML, CSS, and more. Each lesson has been like a stepping stone, bringing me closer to my dream of becoming a tech expert. But my journey doesn't end in the classroom.

Outside of my studies, I'm always exploring new things. Whether it's diving into the world of data science, experimenting with cooking, or getting lost in a good book, I'm always eager to learn and grow.
